portugal

Portugal is a republic in southwestern Europe, bordered by Spain. It has been a member of the European Union since 1986.

Historically, Portugal was a dictatorship until 1975, when it transitioned to a parliamentary democracy. A multi-party electoral system was established, and in 1976, the country adopted a constitution. The portuguese government is divided into 3 branches: the Legislative (the Parliament), the Executive (the Government) and the Judicial (the Courts).

The country has both a president and a prime minister. The president, Marcelo Rebelo de Sousa, serves as the head of the state and holds significant power. While the prime minister, Luís Montenegro, manages the daily operations of the government.
