About this project

The EU Migration Atlas is a student project developed within the minor Cross-Border Migration, Governance and Security at NHL Stenden University of Applied Sciences.

Project context

The project is based on fictional briefing materials inspired by the European Commission’s Directorate-General for Migration and Home Affairs (DG HOME). These materials provide a conceptual foundation for analysing trends, policy developments and governance structures related to migration within the European Union.

This website functions as a prototype platform designed to present accessible, comparative insights into migration policies across EU Member States. The structure, visual design and technical implementation are developed by students for academic purposes only.

Intended audience

This platform is designed for:

  • policymakers and public officials;
  • researchers and students;
  • journalists and civil society organisations;
  • others interested in migration governance in the EU.
