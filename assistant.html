Atlas AI Assistant

The Atlas AI assistant helps users explore migration policies and political climates across EU Member States. You can ask questions about specific countries, compare approaches, or request explanations of key EU frameworks.

What you can do with Atlas AI

  • Ask for a summary of a country’s migration policy and political climate.
  • Compare two Member States across specific themes (asylum, borders, integration).
  • Request clarification on EU frameworks such as CEAS or the Pact on Migration and Asylum.
  • Get short explanations suitable for students, journalists, or policymakers.

Disclaimer: This assistant is a prototype for educational purposes within the minor Cross-Border Migration, Governance & Security. It does not represent an official tool of the European Commission.

Responses are grounded in Atlas markdown sources when available.
