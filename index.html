Key dimensions

What each country profile covers

Every country page in the EU Migration Atlas applies the same structure, making it easier to compare political context, migration trends and concrete policy measures.

PC

Political climate

Overview of the governing coalition, main parties, and how migration is framed in the public and parliamentary debate.

MS

Migration statistics

Key figures on arrivals, asylum applications, recognitions and return, plus routes that are particularly relevant for the country.

CP

Current policies

Summary of the main policy instruments, legal reforms and implementation measures shaping migration and asylum governance.

AP

Application possibilities

High-level overview of entry channels, application procedures and integration opportunities that are relevant for different categories of migrants.

About the Atlas

The EU Migration Atlas brings together key information on migration and asylum policies in EU Member States, helping users compare national approaches and political trends.

What you can explore

Use the country profiles to see how each Member State designs its migration policies, or compare countries side by side to understand broader patterns across the EU.
